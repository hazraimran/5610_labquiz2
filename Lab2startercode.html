5 must-see attraction in Vancouver

  1. Canada Place: A beautiful landmark in Vancouver.
  2. Stanley Park: A 405-hectare park in British Columbia.
  3. Science World: A science center in Vancouver.
  4. Vancouver Art Gallery: A prominent art museum in Vancouver.
  5. Harbour Centre: A skyscraper in downtown Vancouver.

Vancouver is a vibrant city known for its natural beauty, diverse culture, and rich history. It offers world-class cuisine, outdoor adventures, and a thriving arts scene.
